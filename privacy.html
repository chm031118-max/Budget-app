Privacy Policy

The Personal Budget App is a client-side budgeting tool created for coursework demonstration purposes. The app does not require account registration and does not send personal budget data to a server.

1. What We Collect

Browser localStorage is used to store budget entries, language preference, and cookie consent status.

2. Why We Collect It

This data is collected to keep the app functional, remember user preferences, and preserve budget records after refreshing the page.

3. How We Use Data

Stored data is used only to display budget information inside the browser and remember user preferences. The app does not send personal budget data to a server.

4. Cookie Choices

Users can choose Reject, Necessary Only, or Accept All in the cookie consent banner. The selected choice is stored in localStorage as the cookie consent status.

5. Third Parties

The app does not use third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, analytics, profiling, or third-party data sharing.

6. User Control

Users can clear stored data at any time through their browser settings by deleting localStorage for this site.

7. Contact

For coursework-related questions or privacy-related enquiries, please contact the project group through the submitted GitHub repository or the contact information provided in the coursework submission.
